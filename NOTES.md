# Notes

## Known limitations

- **storyboard-sketch references are style-A-leaning.** The `references/*.md` craft files
  carry scope headers bounding them to style A (`rough_notebook_storyboard`), but their
  bodies still read as style-A doctrine (colored-pencil / transparent-background). This is
  acceptable for v1. A future pass could generalize the craft references to be style-neutral
  for heavier use of styles B–F (blueprint, industrial, keynote, whiteboard).

## Backlog

- Split or rename the style-A craft references before relying heavily on styles B–F.
- Add lightweight B–F craft references when those modes become common enough to need more than the locked contracts in `reference.md`.
